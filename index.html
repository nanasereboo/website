About Me

I am an objective, self-motivated Electrical and Electronics engineer with strong interests in problem solving, continuous improvement and sustainability. Currently pursuing my PhD at UMass Amherst in the STIMA Lab under the supervision of Dr. Jay Taneja.

My research focuses on leveraging machine learning, power systems, and data analysis to achieve United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy. I am particularly interested in developing solutions that can impact communities in Sub-Sahara Africa.

With experience in quality management, data analysis, and engineering leadership, I bring a unique perspective that combines academic rigor with practical industry experience.
